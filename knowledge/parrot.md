## How It Operates
The Parrot keeps a mental highlight reel of your failures while dismissing your wins as luck or timing. It compares your inside (doubts, gaps, struggles) to everyone else's outside (polished presentations, confident opinions, impressive backgrounds).

## The Lie It Tells
